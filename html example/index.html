Discrimination, Exclusion & Injustice Task Force

About the taskforce

A task force established to remove Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) protocols within a company is typically formed to realign organizational policies and practices with a merit-based framework. This initiative often involves dismantling DEI offices, eliminating identity-based quotas or objectives, and ceasing related training programs. The overarching goal is to ensure that hiring, promotion, and leadership decisions are based solely on individual qualifications and performance, without consideration of race, gender, or other demographic factors. Such efforts are often framed as a return to equal opportunity principles, aiming to foster a workplace environment where all employees are evaluated and advanced based on merit.â€‹
