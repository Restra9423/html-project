What is InsureMeNot?

InsureMeNot is an American health insurance company dedicated to providing fair and equal access to healthcare. Many insurance companies in the U.S. are out to scam as many people out of the health benefits they've paid exorbitant prices for, but we're not like that at all! We simply want to offer help to people paying for medical coverage at reasonable prices, and we only ask that you can meet our terms and conditions. We still take the money either way.
